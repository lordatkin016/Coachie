About SAMVINSKEYS

Hello! I'm Adesoji Samuel Kayode, also known as SAMVINSKEYS. I'm running for the position of Sports Director for NACOS 009 in the Faculty of Computing at Adekunle Ajasin University, Akungba (AAUA) for the 25/26 session. My passion for sports and student welfare drives me to ensure that our faculty remains at the forefront of campus activities and sports excellence.

  • Provide equal opportunities for all students, regardless of gender or background
  • Collaborate with other faculties to organize inclusive sports events
  • Enhance training facilities and equipment for better performance
  • Ensure fair representation of students' interests in sports decision-making
